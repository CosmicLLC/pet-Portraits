## Variant A — Renaissance Reveal

**Use when**: cold acquisition, any occasion, any persona except memorial.
**Why it works**: pattern-break against competitor line-art ads. The unique Renaissance style + an unwrap-reaction moment creates a scroll-stopper. Pet in velvet robes + gold frame is 100% our territory — no competitor ships it.

**Image prompt skeleton:**
```
Cinematic lifestyle photo of [gifter] in [warm modern interior], mid-reaction to unwrapping a framed gift. Hands to mouth, eyes shimmering, soft smile. Tissue paper mid-air. Partner beside them smiling at the reaction, not at the gift. The gift is an 11×14 framed Renaissance-style oil painting of a [breed] wearing a red velvet royal robe, white lace ruff collar, gold chain, deep burgundy draped background, painted in 17th century Flemish portraiture style. Natural afternoon daylight from left, warm cream and olive palette, subtle gold accents, shallow depth of field, 50mm lens, photoreal, editorial lifestyle, candid but polished.
```

**Copy block:**
- Hook: `"She Saw Him In Oil Paint / And Cried Harder Than / Our Wedding."`
- Sub: `"Real reactions. Real pet moms. 4.9★ from 40,000+ pet parents."`
- CTA: `"Preview Free — 30 Seconds"`
- Badge: `"★ 4.9 · Ships in 3–5 days"`

## Variant B — Four-Style Grid

**Use when**: education / differentiation stage, retargeting anyone who visited but didn't convert.
**Why it works**: leans into our unique four-style range. Competitors ship one style; we ship four. Showing all four in a 2×2 grid reframes choice-overload as variety.

**Image prompt skeleton:**
```
2×2 grid of four framed pet portraits on a cream wall, same [breed] subject in four distinct art styles: watercolor (loose brushwork, pastel), oil painting (Flemish impasto, jewel-toned background), Renaissance (velvet robes, gold leaf), line art (continuous black ink on cream). Each in a thin matching frame, hung close together as a gallery wall. Natural daylight from left, editorial lifestyle, photoreal, Kinfolk aesthetic.
```

**Copy block:**
- Hook: `"I Couldn't Pick. / So I Got All Four."`
- Sub: `"Four artistic styles. One pet. 30-second preview, free."`
- CTA: `"Try All Four Free"`
- Badge: `"★ 4.9 · Ships in 3–5 days"`

## Variant C — Speed Hook (Reels / TikTok)

**Use when**: short-form video placements (TikTok, Reels, Stories, YouTube Shorts).
**Why it works**: our 30-second preview is the one differentiator no competitor can match. The POV-urgent-gifter frame turns a weakness ("I forgot") into a hero moment.

**Storyboard (15–20s):**
- 0–2s: worried gifter face, phone in hand. Text: `"POV: I'm 6 hours from Mother's Day. No gift."`
- 2–4s: screen-record of pawmasterpiece.com opening
- 4–6s: photo upload screen-record
- 6–9s: style picker, tap Watercolor. Countdown text: `"30 seconds"`
- 9–12s: preview animates in. VO: `"Oh my god."`
- 12–15s: cut to canvas on mom's wall next day. Text: `"She thinks I planned this for months."`
- 15–18s: end card. `"pawmasterpiece.com — preview free in 30 seconds"`

**Copy block:**
- Hook: `"POV: I'm 6 hours from Mother's Day. No gift."`
- Pivot: `"30 seconds."`
- Payoff: `"She thinks I planned this for months."`
- End card: `"pawmasterpiece.com — preview free · 3–5 day ship"`

## Hook library

Rotate through these. Don't use any one more than twice per campaign window.

### Reaction-framed (best CTR)
- "I paid $19 for a portrait of my dog and I might cry."
- "If you have a dog, stop what you're doing."
- "My mom's going to lose it when she sees this."
- "Three seconds into this reveal and I screamed."
- "Watch what happens when I upload a photo of my cat."

### Compare-and-win (best for gift campaigns)
- "Flowers wilt. A portrait doesn't."
- "Better than another tie."
- "The gift she'll actually hang on the wall."
- "Not a mug. Not a tote. Actual art."
- "She already has candles and jewelry."

### Speed / urgency (owned territory)
- "30 seconds. That's how long this took."
- "I forgot Mother's Day. This saved me."
- "I sent the gift on Christmas morning. Ship date didn't matter."
- "I figured out what to get my mom who has everything. It took me 30 seconds."

### Memorial (gentle — separate campaign, different creators)
- "A gentle way to keep them close."
- "For when you're ready."
- "Any photo is the right photo."
- NO other memorial hooks. Never pair with discount or countdown language.

## Creative brief for a new variant

When you want to spin a new variant, answer these and deliver:

1. **Persona**: which of the three (gifter / self / memorial)?
2. **Occasion**: evergreen or campaign-specific?
3. **Style**: which of the four portrait styles anchors the hero?
4. **Hook pattern**: reaction / compare-and-win / speed / memorial?
5. **Placement**: 1:1 static / 9:16 video / 4:5 feed / 16:9 desktop?
6. **Banned**: no children, no stopwatches, no competitor mentions, no em-dashes in copy, never the word "AI."

Then output: image prompt, headline, subhead, CTA, badge. Nothing else.
