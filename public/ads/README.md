## Before shipping to Meta / TikTok

1. Open in Figma or Canva
2. Overlay the brand headline: `"She Saw Him In Oil Paint And Cried Harder Than Our Wedding."` (Playfair Display)
3. Subhead: `"Real reactions. Real pet moms. 4.9★ from 40,000+ pet parents."` (DM Sans)
4. CTA button: `"Preview Free — 30 Seconds"` (brand-green fill, white text)
5. Corner badge: `"⭐ 4.9 · Ships in 3–5 days"`
6. Export at the target aspect ratio per placement:
   - Meta feed: 1080×1080 (1:1)
   - Meta Reels / IG Stories / TikTok: 1080×1920 (9:16)
   - Meta feed vertical: 1080×1350 (4:5)
